About me

I am a Robotics and Automation Engineer with a bachelor's degree in Instrumentation and Control and an ongoing master's degree in Mechatronics, Robotics, and Automation Engineering at Arizona State University. I am passionate about advancing technology and solving complex problems with innovative solutions.

As an intern at Tesla, I designed and executed control systems for the Drive Unit Manufacturing, using PLC programming, electrical design, and cutting-edge technologies to reduce costs and environmental impact. I collaborated with a diverse and talented team of engineers and technicians, supporting Tesla's mission of accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy. I also gained proficiency in C, C++, C#, Python, HTML, JavaScript, and TypeScript, and honed my skills in MATLAB Simulink, DeltaV Explorer, Ignition 8.1, and more.

At ASU, I have engineered various academic projects, such as a compact IIoT control system with AWS Cloud integration, a text-to-speech system for OCR-generated text, a ROSMASTER X3 ROS robot, and a self-balancing motorcycle robot. I am eager to learn new skills and explore innovative opportunities in the robotics and automation field. My goal is to apply my knowledge and experience to create positive impact and value for society and the environment.
